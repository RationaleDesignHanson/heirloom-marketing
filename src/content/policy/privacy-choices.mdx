# User Privacy Choices

_Last updated: {meta.lastUpdated}_

This page explains the privacy choices available to users of Heirloom Recipe Box (“Heirloom”).

## 1) Your choices in the app

### A) Sharing controls
Heirloom is private by default. Where available, you can control who can see a recipe using visibility settings (for example: Private / Shared / Public).

### B) Analytics controls (if available)
If analytics controls are available in your build, you can manage them in **Settings**.

## 2) Request account deletion

### In-app deletion (preferred)
1. Open **Heirloom**.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Tap **Account** (or **Privacy**) → **Delete Account**.
4. Follow the confirmation steps.

### Email deletion (if you can’t access the app)
Email **support@heirloomrecipebox.app** with the subject line **“Account Deletion Request”** and include the email address associated with your account.

For safety, we may ask you to confirm the request from the email address on the account.

## 3) Request access/export of your data
Email **support@heirloomrecipebox.app** with the subject line **“Data Export Request”** and include the email address associated with your account.

## 4) Contact
Questions about privacy choices: **support@heirloomrecipebox.app**.
