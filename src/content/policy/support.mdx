# Support

Need help with Heirloom? Here are the fastest ways to get support, plus account, subscription, and privacy options.

## Contact support

### Email (best for App Store review)
Email: **support@heirloomrecipebox.app**

If you’re contacting us about your account, include the email address you use to sign in.

### Press
Press: **press@heirloomrecipebox.app**

### General
General: **hello@heirloomrecipebox.app**

## Fastest help: Discord (beta community)
Join the **Heirloom Beta Testing** Discord to:
- report bugs
- request features
- share reproduction steps/screenshots
- talk recipes with other testers

**Join here:** https://discord.gg/tfrMzefJFj

### Suggested channels (optional setup)
- `#welcome`
- `#support`
- `#bug-reports`
- `#feature-requests`
- `#recipe-sharing`

### What to include (so we can fix it fast)
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Screenshots or a screen recording (if possible)
- Device model + iOS version
- Whether you were importing from link / video / PDF / camera / voice

## Troubleshooting (quick fixes)

### Imports are stuck / not completing
- Check you have a stable network connection.
- Try restarting the app.
- Try saving a different recipe URL to confirm the issue isn’t source-specific.

### Share Extension isn’t showing in Safari
- Open the iOS Share Sheet → **More** → enable **Heirloom**.
- Restart Safari and try again.

## TestFlight feedback (if you’re in beta)
Open **TestFlight → Heirloom → Send Beta Feedback** and attach screenshots/logs if available.

## How to Delete Your Account
If you want to delete your account and associated data:

1. Open **Heirloom** on your iPhone.
2. Go to **Settings** (inside the app).
3. Tap **Account** (or **Privacy**) → **Delete Account**.
4. Follow the confirmation steps.

If you can’t access the app, email **support@heirloomrecipebox.app** with the subject line:
**“Account Deletion Request”**  
Include the email address associated with your Heirloom account.

> Note: For safety, we may ask you to confirm the request from the email address on the account.

If you can’t access the email address on the account, we may be unable to verify ownership and complete deletion.

## Data export / access request
To request an export of your account data, email **support@heirloomrecipebox.app** with the subject:
**“Data Export Request”** and include the email address associated with your account.

## How to Cancel a Subscription
Subscriptions are billed through Apple. To cancel:

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID** (your name at the top)
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Select **Heirloom Recipe Box**
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

If you started a trial, canceling stops the subscription from renewing at the end of the trial period.

## Refunds
Apple handles refunds for App Store purchases. You can request a refund through Apple’s purchase history (the exact flow varies by region and iOS version).
